WELOME TO PARISONS

We are one of the largest food processing companies based in South India. Our activities touch all aspects of the
food supply chain from manufacturing, R&D, services, logistics, trading and retailing. Major food items we deal in
are wheat, edible oil and tea.

OUR PRODUCTS


Our products range from wheat flour, varieties of edible oils to tea. We follow international
standards in quality and source the best crops directly from the source of origin. We give
you good quality products by preserving the goodness of nature.

AREAS WE SERVE

Parisons is one of the largest manufacturer in South India. Our product portfolio includes wheat flour, edible oils, specialty fats, rubber and tea.

Wheat Products

We are one of the biggest processors of wheat in South India. Our wheat purchasing capacity combined with the state of art technology plant ensures consumer gets the best quality wheat flour, with a delicate balance of essential nutrients.

Edible Oil

Our Edible oils are sourced directly from the place of origin. The oils are crushed/refined in our modern plant complying with international standards in quality management. This helps us to deliver the best quality cooking oil.

vanaspaty and specialty fat

The expertise in R&D combined with the craftsmanship of our bakers have produced the finest shortening and margarines in the region. Our specialty fats and vanaspathy’s are the secret ingredient of top bakers.

Tea

Our quality teas are grown in the pristine forested hills of Waynad. Nurtured by the pleasant atmosphere and chilly mists of the mountains, the teas are mild tasting with a clean fragrance. We own four tea plantations in Wayanad district, Kerala.

Bakery ingredient

More than any other dessert, a cake signifies celebration and the important moments in life. That’s why Parisons Group has developed a range of specialty cake mixes with their new brand Named Prime . Our cakes not only taste delicious, they give your bakery a platform for growth and long-term success.
